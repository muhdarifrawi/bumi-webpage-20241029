What Is Art Therapy

A type of therapeutic practice where creating art - drawing, painting or sculpting - is used to allow people to express themselves and explore their inner feelings.

Art Therapy focuses on using creative processes as a means for self-expression and healing, especially if one finds it hard to put their thoughts into words.

Led by a trained profesional, who guides particiapnts to use art to process emotions, work through stressors, or gain insights into themselves in a private, safe and supportive environment.
